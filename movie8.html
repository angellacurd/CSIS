Movie Description In the movie "Hitch", Alex Hitchens is a professional dating consultant. He gives guidance to men who needs skills that flatter the ladies. Albert is one of his clients and is trying to win the heart of Allegra. As he mentors Alex, he meets this girl, Sara, at a bar. Alex saves her from another mans pick-up lines. As the story progresses, Alex and Sara have the most unromantic dates. They still fall for each other. Sara is a reporter for a tabloid magazine and finds out about a man who is called the Date Doctor. She tries to ruin his reputation after her friend has a bad encounter with a man that refers to the Date Doctor. Sara ends up finding out that the Date Doctor was actually Alex. They battle back and forth and it almost ruins their relationship, but eventually get back together.
