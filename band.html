Popular Albums

  • Core
    Core is Stone Temple Pilot's debut album. It was released in 1992. Core is the band's best selling album. It reached number 1 on the Billboard Heatseekers Charts. The song "Plush" won the 1994 Grammy Award for Best Hard Rock Performance.
  • Purple
    In 1994, Purple was the second studio album released. This album was a success for Stone Temple Pilots as it debuted at number 1 on the Billboard 200 chart. There were over six million copies sold. Popular songs Vasoline and Interstate Love Song were on the album.
  • Tiny Music... Songs from a Vatican Gift Shop
    The third release, Tiny Music... Songs from a Vatican Gift Shop, was released in 1996. The band had to take time off when front man Scott Weiland was arrested for drug possession in 1995, but reunited afterwards to record this album. This was the first album to start the movement from the bands original grunge sound. The album ended up only reaching number 4 on the bilboard top 200.
