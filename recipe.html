Better Than Sex Cake

This cake is not the cake that you want to eat if you are looking to cutting calories. The cake is filled with caramel and is one of the most moist cakes you will devour. It is topped with a lite whipped cream and crunchy candy. It is best when refrigerated overnight.


  • 1 box Devil's Food Cake Mix
  • 1 8-oz tub Cool Whip
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 bottle caramel ice cream topping
  • 1 package Heath bar pieces

    1. Cook Devil's Food cake mix according to package directions. Use a 9x13 inch pan.
    2. Pull cake from oven and poke holes into the cake with the end of a wooden spoon.
    3. Open the can of sweetened condensed milk and pour over the top of the cake and directly into the holes.
    4. Repeat the previous step with the caramel ice cream topping.
    5. Let the cake sit and cool until it is safe to store in the refrigerator.
    6. Refrigerate the cake overnight or until the cake is cold.
    7. When cake is completely cooled, remove from refrigerator and spread Cool Whip topping onto the cake.
    8. The final step before serving is to top the cake with the Heath bar pieces. Use the entire bag and spread evenly.
